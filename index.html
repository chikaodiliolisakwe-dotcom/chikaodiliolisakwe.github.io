About

In my most recent role, I was the only SDR. There was no outbound playbook, no email sequences, and no real process, so I put everything together, tried different things, and kept changing what wasn't working until it started getting replies and booking meetings.

I've worked with teams reaching people in the US, UK, Canada, Australia, Israel, and other countries. I use HubSpot, Apollo, Sales Navigator, and Instantly to find prospects, keep my pipeline organized, and run outbound campaigns.

What I enjoy most is trying to understand people before I reach out. I like researching companies, writing cold emails that don't sound like a template, and finding better ways to start conversations.

If something is missing, I don't wait for someone else to fix it. I like to figure it out, put something in place, and keep making it better as I learn. I enjoy challenging myself to find better ways to do things.

I also believe people come first. When people feel supported, they do better work, and good work leads to better results.
